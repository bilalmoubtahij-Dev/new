# Highway Academy: English Assessment System Overview

This document provides a high-level, business-focused breakdown of how the English Placement Test works on your website. It explains the mechanics of the test, how grades are automatically calculated, and how those grades map directly to international standards like IELTS and TOEFL to help you perfectly position your students.

## 1. How the Test Works (The Student Experience)

We have built a robust 30-question test designed to evaluate a student's true English proficiency. Unlike basic online forms, this system is highly interactive to prevent guessing:

*   **Standard Selection:** Basic multiple-choice questions to test core grammar principles.
*   **Dictionary-Verified Typing:** When students are asked to type a word, the system runs an invisible check against thousands of real English dictionary words. If a student tries to cheat or skip the question by mashing keys (e.g., typing "asdfghi"), the system rejects it immediately.
*   **Drag & Drop Ordering:** Students must physically re-arrange scrambled words on the screen to form grammatically correct sentences.
*   **Interactive Matching grids:** Students tap a vocabulary word, then tap a definition to draw connecting arrows. 

These sophisticated tools make the test feel incredibly premium and accurate, drastically elevating your Academy's brand reputation.

---

## 2. How the Grading Engine Works

The test operates on a strict **100% scale**. Because there are exactly 30 questions, each correct answer is worth exactly `~3.33%`. 

The automated grading engine is unforgiving with mistakes:
*   In **Drag & Drop**, the sequence must be 100% correct, or it scores a 0.
*   In **Matching**, every single line drawn between words and definitions must be correct.
*   In **Text Input**, spelling must be flawless, though the system is forgiving about accidental capitalization (ignoring uppercase vs. lowercase).

When the student finishes question #30, the system instantly tallies their score and reveals their official level.

## 3. Scoring Standards: CEFR, IELTS, and TOEFL

## 4. Business Value & Driving Enrollments

The ultimate goal of this test is not just to give the student a number, but to **drive sales and reservations for Highway Academy**.

Here is the automated "funnel" flow the system executes:
1.  **Engagement:** The student takes the test for free, staying highly engaged with the premium Drag & Drop and Matching screens.
2.  **Instant Authority:** The student is presented with a beautiful, animated score screen evaluating their CEFR capability.
3.  **The Upsell:** Based strictly on their score (from A1 to C1), the system generates a tailored recommendation text (e.g., *"You are fluent in many situations. We recommend our General English (B2) Program"*).
4.  **Auto-Routing:** When the student clicks "View Recommended Courses", the system automatically carries their specific English level in the background and sends them to your Programs page. When they finally click "Reserve Your Spot", the system can use that data to securely know exactly where to place them without requiring a manual evaluation by your staff.
